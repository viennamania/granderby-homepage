How to Play

Own Racehorses and manage them
Anytime, Anywhere!

The GRANDERBY management app provides various services to players who own GRANDERBY NFTs.

You can easily manage horse, racetrack, and jockey NFTs by categorizing them.
In addition, based on the information of my NFT horse, you can enjoy a realistic game that incorporates actual horse racing elements such as checking the condition of horses participating in races, training, and breeding systems in real games.

Players can easily purchase NFTs and immediately jump into the game through the market service provided by the app.

Experience systematic and dynamic Real-Time racing in the GRANDERBY! Download Now!

GRANDERBY is a game where players raise and manage NFT horses to enter them into races and earn tokens.

Players have the opportunity to develop their own horses into top racehorses and earn game tokens.

Turn your dreams into reality with NFT horses in GRANDERBY! Experience the best Horse Racing through raising and management, and The thrill of watching your tokens stack up awaits you.

Things You Can Be in the GRANDERBY

  • Horse Owner

    Players who own NFT horses can become Horse Owners. As a Horse Owner, care for your horses and raise them into top racehorses through breeding. Additionally, achieving good results in races allows you to earn more GRANDERBY game tokens.

  • Track Owner

    Purchase stakes in racetracks and become a Track Owner. You have the opportunity to earn game tokens as part of your race entry fee in game tokens.

  • Jockey

    Players can have additional jockeys with special abilities apart from the standard jockey. Win more races with the jockey who has the best combination with your NFT horse.

Play GRANDERBY and Get Reward

Become a racehorse trainer, a jockey, or a track owner and experience the vibrant world of horse racing firsthand!

NFT Benefit

  • 1. Race Track

    Imagine the cheering crowds at your race track, shouting with excitement. If you own stakes in a racetrack, you have a chance to earn a portion of the racing profits.

  • 2. Race Horse

    Horses are the key to your game. The NFT you choose will compete in real races. Elevate the abilities of your horse to their highest potential. Train, nurture, and breed your horse to the top of the podium and enjoy the moment of victory.

  • 3. Jockey

    Racehorses and jockeys go hand in hand. Purchase your desired jockeys or pay jockey fees to other players jockey to participate in the game. Scrutinize the jockeys’ abilities closely and emerge as a racing expert.
